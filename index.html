Algorithmic in-house DJ

Deadbullet Music employs artificial intelligence algorithms to search the internet for trending songs over a variety of sources.
Our algorthmic artificial intelligence then queues up the song into the regular playlist when requested.
Did we also mention, we don't cut the last 10 - 60 seconds off the end of the song? While other services do this, we don't.
Deadbullet Music prefers to play the full song, so your customers can get the full immersive music experience.
